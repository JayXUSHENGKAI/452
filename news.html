Huawei Mate 70 Series

This series includes Mate 70, Mate 70 Pro, and Mate 70 Pro+. The Mate 70 standard version is equipped with a 6.69-inch 1.5K straight screen and supports side fingerprint unlocking. The lens module is a centered large-circle triple camera, and it also supports wireless charging and high-specification dust and water resistance. The Mate 70 Pro screen is upgraded to 6.88 inches with an equal-depth four-curved screen design, and it supports side fingerprint unlocking and 3D face recognition. The Mate 70 Pro+ has hardware configurations basically the same as the Pro version, with some small upgrades in some configurations.

Envisioning the Future of Technology

The Development of Mobile Phones in the Future

In the future, mobile phone designs are likely to become even more diverse and innovative. We may witness the emergence of truly foldable and rollable phones that offer larger screen sizes when needed while remaining compact and portable. These flexible displays could revolutionize the way we interact with our devices, allowing for seamless multitasking and enhanced viewing experiences, such as watching movies or reading e-books without the limitations of a fixed screen size.
